Do It Yourself

Rustic Wooden Coffee Table

I built this rustic farmhouse coffee table using some basic materials: 4 x 2x6 boards (8 feet long), 1 x 2x4 board (8 feet long), 3 x 2x2 furring strips (8 feet long), 100 x 2-1/2" self-tapping screws (picked these up from Home Depot), wood glue, and corner braces for extra support. Building something from scratch always feels pretty satisfying—it’s not just about putting pieces together, but about making something that’s completely your own. There’s a real sense of accomplishment in taking raw materials and turning them into something functional and good-looking. Plus, every time I use the table, I’m reminded that I built it myself, and that’s a pretty cool feeling.
